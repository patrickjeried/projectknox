About Us

Founded in 2012

Mission

To be known as the best technical institution in the Philippines, value knowledge and skills that will value knowledge and skills coming from the small municipality of Pandi, Bulacan. Proud as it may seems but, our professors and instructors are "TOPNOTCH" in their field of endeavors!

Vision

Our vision at Knox is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate
and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practise the core values of the school: respect and excellence.
